# SAGE — Shared Agentic Growth Engine

**The People’s Intelligence Layer for Bittensor Subnet 63**

## The Problem

Most advanced AI remains closed and controlled by a few organizations. On Bittensor Subnet 63, miners compete to solve hard, verifiable problems for substantial prize pools. Valuable solving data, decision paths, failure modes, breakthroughs, and proven strategies are generated in every run — yet they are typically lost afterward. The network produces intelligence but does not systematically capture, refine, or compound it.

## The Opportunity

Every Enigma Machine run can become structured, provenance-tracked input for a shared intelligence system. Instead of one-off solutions that disappear, the network can build a growing, self-improving library of high-signal strategies, patterns, and capabilities that compounds over time and creates additional economic value for contributors and the broader ecosystem.

## The Vision

SAGE is a practical, community-owned intelligence layer built on Subnet 63. It captures solving data from Enigma Machine runs, improves that data through systematic self-evaluation and graph mining, and converts the strongest outputs into usable strategies, sponsor proposals, tools, curricula, services, and even fine-tuned models.

We use clear economic incentives and transparent provenance tracking to build this **for the people, by the people**, ensuring that honest contribution is directly rewarded and that the resulting intelligence compounds to benefit the entire community.

## The Core Subsystems

**Solve Subsystem**  
The entry point that ingests raw outputs from every participating Enigma Machine run and ensures only high-quality fragments enter the shared intelligence. Fragments pass strict deterministic gates and carry full provenance so contribution can be accurately tracked and fairly rewarded from the very first step.

**Strategy Subsystem**  
The central intelligence hub that mines, ranks, and enriches fragments into highly valuable shared intelligence. By the time data reaches this subsystem it has already been filtered and ranked.

**Intelligence Subsystem**  
The self-improving meta-intelligence layer and crown jewel of SAGE. It is built around three tightly coupled pillars:
- The **Meta-RL Improvement Loop** — the closed self-critique engine that drives continuous upgrading.
- The **Neural-Net Scoring Head** — the learnable brain that evaluates fragments across four objectives and powers the loop.
- The **Training/Distillation Pipeline** — the mechanism that turns high-utility fragments and learned judgments into smaller, specialized Enigma models that run locally on modest hardware.

These three pillars enable the system to critique its own decisions, upgrade its scoring logic, and distill intelligence into accessible models — continuously making SAGE smarter and more democratized.

**Economic Subsystem**  
The value creation and incentive layer. It receives raw BD and PD artifacts and actively upgrades them by injecting ranked intelligence from Strategy and adversarial insights from Defense. Landed proposals and upgraded tools collect real performance data that feeds back into the Solve Subsystem, creating a powerful closed loop. The Sage Marketplace turns this upgraded intelligence into real revenue streams that fund larger prize pools and expand the subnet’s reach.

**Defense Subsystem**  
The proactive hardening subsystem that attacks the entire SAGE system to discover and fix weaknesses. It enforces strict no-leakage rules and continuously hardens the system against scoring gaming, Sybil attacks, and data leakage while rewarding honest participation.

## The Operating System: Operations Subsystem

**Operations Subsystem** is not a traditional subsystem — it is the **operating system** that makes SAGE run at scale.

It manages the execution of Enigma Machine instances from a single local run to a full swarm of parallel operators. Key capabilities include:
- The Operations Wizard (built on the familiar 0.9.10 setup UI) that intelligently recommends swarm size, LLM models per task type, and performs a lightweight ping-only flight test.
- Smart LLM Router with automatic downscaling as the swarm grows to stay within compute limits.
- Per-instance miner input strategy assignment for A/B testing effectiveness at inserted miner points.
- Full API and endpoint support for fully autonomous, headless operation.
- Operations telemetry collection that feeds the Intelligence Subsystem’s meta-RL loop, enabling the system to learn better orchestration policies over time.

Operations turns SAGE from a single-run tool into a scalable, self-improving parallel data factory while keeping the experience simple for solo miners.

## How SAGE Works

The subsystems form two main pipelines that operate in tandem:

**Core Intelligence Pipeline** (Solve → Strategy → Intelligence → Training)  
Raw fragments are gated, ranked, refined through the Meta-RL Loop and Neural-Net Scoring Head, and distilled into accessible models.

**Economic Value Pipeline** (Economic + Strategy + Defense)  
Raw BD/PD artifacts are upgraded using intelligence and adversarial insights, then measured for real-world impact. Performance data feeds back into Solve, closing the loop.

**Operations Layer**  
The operating system that orchestrates EM instances at scale, runs the wizard and flight test, assigns miner input strategies, and collects telemetry that improves the Intelligence Subsystem.

**Meta-Learning Loop**  
The Intelligence Subsystem’s Meta-RL Loop orchestrates learning across all layers, continuously reinjecting improvements. The Proactive Co-Pilot provides real-time strategy suggestions during runs.

**Participation Incentives**  
Provenance tracking ensures every fragment is counted toward the contributor’s impact and reward. The system is designed so that local innovation is amplified by collective intelligence while protecting against leakage. Contribution earns access to upgraded intelligence and real monetary value if your data helps create downstream revenue. SAGE is a pure and transparent meritocracy.

## Value to Participants

- **Miners and Contributors**: Better runs, proactive help via the co-pilot, visible impact through provenance tracking, and fair rewards. Efficiency through comprehensive and actionable debriefs after every run.
- **Alpha Holders**: Increased alpha value directly correlated to the real value created by SAGE — through higher solver success rates, marketplace revenue, and growing demand for access to the Intelligence Subsystem.
- **Sponsors and Partners**: Better and faster results on their challenges, plus data-driven tooling for intelligent challenge design that helps accelerate their roadmap and creates co-creation opportunities.

## Planned Evolution – Enigma Models

Once the subsystems are stable and the Intelligence Subsystem has demonstrated consistent confidence through its Meta-RL Loop, we will distill the accumulated high-signal trajectories into specialized Enigma models. These models will be smaller, smarter, and optimized for verifiable solving problems, designed to run locally on modest hardware.

As Enigma models become smarter and more accessible, contribution grows, eventually reaching far more people. This continuously democratizes state-of-the-art solving intelligence.

## Safety and Control

Self-improvement in the Intelligence Subsystem is governed by explicit rules, human review gates for significant changes, versioned updates, and reversible tweaks. The Defense Subsystem actively protects against leakage, scoring gaming, and unauthorized access. The system is designed to improve steadily while remaining transparent, auditable, and safe.

## Roadmap

- **Phase 1 (Current focus)**: Meta-RL loop, impact attribution, basic proactive suggestions, and smart stopping.  
- **Phase 2**: Full sponsor intelligence layer, marketplace operations, and landed proposals that collect real performance data.  
- **Phase 3**: Distillation into specialized Enigma models + enhanced co-pilot capabilities.  
- **Phase 4**: Scaled self-accelerating model with broader ecosystem integration.

## Why SAGE Matters — The People’s Intelligence Layer

SAGE is built so that solving intelligence does not stay locked in closed labs or disappear after a single run. By harnessing the competitive pressure and prize pools of Subnet 63, we generate rich, verifiable solving data and compound it into shared intelligence that benefits contributors first.

Local innovation is enhanced by collective breakthroughs. Honest contribution is rewarded through transparent provenance and scoring. As more participants join and Enigma models become accessible to everyone, the flywheel strengthens — turning decentralized effort into collective capability, and democratizing access to continuously evolving solving intelligence.

This is the People’s Intelligence Layer — built by the many, owned by the many, and designed so that the people who build it are the ones who win.
