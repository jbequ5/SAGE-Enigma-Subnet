## Role in SAGE
The Economic Subsystem is the value creation and economic capstone of SAGE. It takes raw BD/PD artifacts generated by local Enigma Machine runs and upgrades them into higher-value, monetizable outputs (sponsor proposals, tools, curricula, services) using intelligence from Strategy and Defense. It then routes these outputs through the Sage Marketplace to generate real revenue that funds larger prize pools and expands the subnet.

## How it Works
The upgrade process is controlled and scripted:

**Upgrade Algorithm (pseudocode)**:
1. Receive raw BD/PD artifact from a local run.
2. Query Strategy Subsystem for the top-k most relevant ranked fragments (using task/domain matching + RankScore).
3. Query Defense Subsystem for relevant adversarial examples and hardening rules.
4. Synapse (Meta-Agent) injects proven strategies, verifier rules, and economic impact data into the artifact.
5. Produce upgraded version and run internal validation (7D verifier self-check + marketplace readiness check).
6. Measure expected revenue potential and downstream EFS impact.
7. Route to Sage Marketplace for monetization.

**Contribution Tracking and Rewards**:
Every upgraded artifact credits original contributors through immutable provenance and the contribution scoring formula. Participants see exactly how their fragments helped generate economic value and receive fair rewards.

## Key Formulas
**ContributionScore** (used across Economic and other subsystems):
$$ \text{ContributionScore} = 0.40 \cdot \text{EFS}_{60/40} + 0.25 \cdot \text{utilization} + 0.20 \cdot \text{graph\_centrality} + 0.15 \cdot \text{refined\_value\_added} $$

**RewardShare** for marketplace revenue is proportional to ContributionScore, with a minimum floor for honest contributors.

## Example End-to-End Flow
A miner submits a raw sponsor proposal for a quantum error correction challenge. Economic queries Strategy for the highest-ranked fragments on stabilizer codes and verifier patterns, pulls hardening rules from Defense, and Synapse upgrades the proposal into a complete, verifiable package with embedded test suites. The upgraded proposal lands with a sponsor, generates revenue, and the new challenge data flows back into Solve — closing the loop and increasing prize pools.

## Integration with Other Subsystems
- **Strategy**: Supplies ranked intelligence for upgrades
- **Defense**: Supplies adversarial examples and hardening rules
- **Intelligence**: Provides improved scoring head and distilled models that make upgrades higher quality
- **Solve**: Receives new high-value challenges generated from landed proposals
- **Synapse**: Orchestrates the upgrade step and surfaces marketplace opportunities

## Outputs and Flywheel Contribution
- Upgraded BD/PD artifacts
- Landed sponsor proposals
- Marketplace revenue that funds larger prize pools
- Real performance data that feeds back into Solve and strengthens the entire flywheel

## No Leakage and Strong Protection
High-value upgraded artifacts are protected by tiered access and selective encryption. Only participating miners and authorized high-contribution users can access them inside their local Enigma Machine runs. All access is logged and auditable.

The Economic Subsystem is the capstone that turns collective intelligence into real economic value. By upgrading artifacts with proven strategies and hardening them against attacks, it creates sponsor proposals and tools that generate revenue, expand the subnet, and increase alpha value — while ensuring honest contributors are fairly rewarded. This is what makes the flywheel self-sustaining and turns the People’s Intelligence Layer into an economic engine that benefits the entire community.
